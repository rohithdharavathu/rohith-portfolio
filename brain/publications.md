# Research Publications

## Publication 1
**Title**: Deep Learning-Based Approaches for Text Classification in NLP: A Comparative Study
**Venue**: IEEE International Conference on Electronics, Computing and Communication Technologies (CONECCT)
**Year**: 2023
**Publisher**: IEEE
**Description**: A systematic comparative study of deep learning architectures for NLP text classification tasks. Evaluated LSTM, BiLSTM, CNN-LSTM, and transformer-based approaches across multiple benchmark datasets. Contributed insights on architecture selection based on dataset size and domain characteristics.

## Publication 2
**Title**: Intelligent Adaptive Learning Systems Using Reinforcement Learning and Knowledge Graphs
**Venue**: Springer International Conference on Intelligent Computing and Applications (ICICA)
**Year**: 2023
**Publisher**: Springer
**Description**: Proposed an adaptive learning system architecture that combines reinforcement learning for personalized content sequencing with knowledge graphs for tracking learner progress and skill dependencies. This research directly informed the design of the Agentic Learning System personal project.

## Publication 3
**Title**: Conversational AI for Financial Services: Intent Recognition and Slot Filling with Transformer Models
**Venue**: IEEE Conference on Computational Intelligence and Applications
**Year**: 2024
**Publisher**: IEEE
**Description**: Developed and evaluated a conversational AI framework for financial services applications. Focused on robust intent recognition and slot filling for banking domain queries. Proposed a hybrid approach combining fine-tuned transformers with rule-based fallbacks for production reliability. Related to research interests in conversational AI for financial services.
