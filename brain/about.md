# About Rohith Dharavathu

## Summary
Rohith Dharavathu is a Data Scientist at HDFC Bank on the WhatsApp Banking & Digital Journeys team. He holds a B.Tech in Computer Science with an AI specialization from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. He has three peer-reviewed publications in IEEE and Springer conferences. He is currently building CodeSage AI — a GitHub-native codebase intelligence system with a live code knowledge graph and persistent multi-agent workflows.

His technical focus is on systems that actually think: multi-agent architectures, logical retrieval, and production ML/AI pipelines. He believes in learning through building and has a bias for doing over planning.
